Hi, I'm Girard.

Content Strategist · Senior Editor · Writer

I am a Sydney-based communications specialist with over 14 years of experience turning complex information into compelling narratives.

My career began in the world of journalism, writing for publications like Vice Australia. Today, I use that same rigorous, investigative approach to help executives, public institutions, and leading brands—like Salesforce, Vimeo, and nbn—communicate with clarity and authority.

Whether it’s navigating a sensitive media environment, managing a multi-channel content rollout, or writing an executive speech, my goal is always the same: to deliver the right message to the right audience.

But everyone in comms says some variation of that, no? I believe to be compelling, you have to sound like you, and you can't say what others are saying. That's why I help my clients be different. Not in a “move fast and break things” way, but in a “let’s just check if the conventional wisdom is hurting us” way.
